5. Sports & Recreation

Sports play a vital role in the holistic development of engineering students, contributing to physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and leadership skills. Engineering colleges today place a strong emphasis on creating a balanced environment where academic excellence is complemented by active participation in sports and games.

Most engineering institutions are equipped with modern sports infrastructure, including playgrounds and courts for Volleyball, Basketball, Football, Cricket, Kabaddi, Tennis, Badminton, and athletics, along with facilities for indoor games such as Table Tennis, Chess, and Carrom.

Colleges actively organize intra- and inter-college sports events, annual sports meets, and coaching camps. Many engineering colleges have dedicated Physical Directors and coaches to train and guide students in various sports disciplines.

  • Facilities for indoor and outdoor games: cricket, volleyball, badminton, table tennis, etc.
  • Gymnasium equipped for fitness and wellness.

Central Library & Learning Resources

The Central Library serves as the academic heart of the campus, offering a rich collection of textbooks, reference materials, national and international journals, e-resources, and digital learning platforms. Equipped with a spacious reading hall, high-speed Wi-Fi, and computerized library management, it provides students and faculty with seamless access to knowledge. Dedicated study zones, reprographic services, and licensed e-library subscriptions support research, innovation, and continuous learning, making it a vibrant hub for academic excellence.

The Central Library is the information hub of the institute, serving as a major learning and resource centre. It plays a creative and innovative role in supporting the teaching, learning, research, and scholarly activities of the campus. With a rapidly growing collection in both print and digital formats and state-of-the-art facilities, the library contributes significantly to the institute’s vision of becoming a world-class academic centre.

Located on the first floor, the library offers a pleasant and inspiring ambience. The well-furnished, well-lit interiors create an atmosphere of serenity, providing students and faculty with an ideal space for focused learning and research. The spacious reading area further enhances the environment for productive study.

Access to Library till 10.00 P.M. all 7 days

The library houses an impressive collection of 42,760 books across 23,013 titles, covering diverse disciplines such as Engineering, Management, Architecture & Design, and Basic Sciences & Humanities. The resources also include a wide range of reference books, 3,179 CDs/DVDs, 134 print journals, and 100 magazines in both print and electronic formats.

The digital library provides access to major e-resources such as IEEE Journals, ScienceDirect, ASME, ASCE, ACM Digital Library, ProQuest Academic Complete e-Book Collection, ProQuest Central e-Journals, and Web of Science. Secure remote access with federated search ensures that users can seamlessly access digital resources anytime, anywhere.
