Clinical Risk Matrix

Tailored management protocols based on predictive risk percentile.

HIGH RISK (>75th Percentile)

Intensive Management

  • Target HbA1c <7.0%. Aggressive glucose control.
  • Initiate Cardioprotection: ACE/ARB, SGLT2i/GLP-1RA, statins.
  • Specialist Referral: Quarterly monitoring (Ophthalmology/Nephrology).
  • Technology: Recommend Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM).
MODERATE RISK (25th-75th Percentile)

Standard/Optimized Care

  • Target HbA1c <7.5%. Optimize current medications.
  • Annual Screening: Standard complications checks.
  • Lifestyle: Structured dietary and exercise programs.
  • Monitoring: Biannual beyondA1C reassessment.
LOW RISK (<25th Percentile)

Maintenance & Prevention

  • Maintain Control: Continue current management strategy.
  • Annual Checkup: Standard annual physical and lab work.
  • Focus: Reinforced education on prevention.
  • Reassessment: Annual beyondA1C monitoring.
